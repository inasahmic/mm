What is Behavioral Therapy?

Behavioral therapy is one of the best-researched forms of therapy and offers clear, everyday tools to specifically address psychological stress. It focuses on changing patterns of thinking, feeling and acting that block or burden you in life.

Together we look at which automatic thoughts and reactions have crept into your life, which inner beliefs influence your self-image and your relationships, and how you can develop healthier, empowering patterns step by step.

The Martial Arts Connection

Just as martial arts require discipline, practice, and gradual improvement, behavioral therapy helps you develop new patterns through consistent practice and mindful awareness of your thoughts and behaviors.
