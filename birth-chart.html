The Reading Process

A structured approach to unlocking your astrological insights

1

Initial Consultation

We begin with a conversation about your current life situation, challenges, and what you hope to gain from the birth chart reading. This helps me tailor the interpretation to your specific needs.

2

Chart Calculation & Analysis

I calculate your precise birth chart using your exact birth time, date, and location. This creates a unique astrological blueprint that reveals your personality structure and life patterns.

3

Psychological Interpretation

Using depth psychology principles, I interpret your chart focusing on inner dynamics, emotional development, and unconscious patterns rather than predictions or fortune-telling.

4

Integration & Application

We discuss how to apply these insights in your daily life, creating practical strategies for personal growth and self-acceptance based on your astrological profile.
